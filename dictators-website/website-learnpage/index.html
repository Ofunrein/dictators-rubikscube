Fundamentals

Cube Notation

Before we start solving, learn the shorthand that describes every possible move on the cube.

Face Moves

Each letter represents a 90° clockwise turn of that face. Add ' for counter-clockwise, or 2 for 180°.

RRight
LLeft
UUp
DDown
FFront
BBack

Modifiers

Combine face letters with these modifiers to describe every possible move.

RClockwise 90° turn of the right face
R'Counter-clockwise 90° turn ("R prime")
R2180° turn (direction doesn't matter)

The Method

7 Steps to Solved

Follow each step in order. By the end, you'll have a fully solved cube.

1
EasyFirst Layer

White Cross

Start by forming a plus sign (+) on the white face. Match each edge piece's second color to the center of its adjacent face.

// white_cross.gif

Tips

  • Always start with white — it's convention.
  • Hold the cube with white center on top.
  • Don't worry about corners yet.
2
EasyFirst Layer

White Corners

Insert the four corner pieces to complete the first layer.

Key Algorithm

R U R' U'Right Trigger
3
MediumSecond Layer

Second Layer Edges

Flip the cube so white is on the bottom. Insert middle-layer edge pieces.

Key Algorithms

U R U' R' U' F' U FInsert Right
U' L' U L U F U' F'Insert Left
4
MediumLast Layer

Yellow Cross

Form a yellow cross on the top face.

Key Algorithm

F R U R' U' F'FRU
5
HardLast Layer

Yellow Face

Orient all yellow corners so the entire top face is yellow.

Key Algorithm

R U R' U R U2 R'Sune
6
HardLast Layer

Position Yellow Corners

Move yellow corners to their correct positions.

Key Algorithm

U R U' L' U R' U' LCorner Swap
7
HardLast Layer

Position Yellow Edges

The final step! Cycle the last-layer edges into place.

Key Algorithms

R U' R U R U R U' R' U' R2CW Cycle
R2 U R U R' U' R' U' R' U R'CCW Cycle
🎉 Congratulations — the cube is solved!
